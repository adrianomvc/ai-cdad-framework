# Brownfield Analysis Skill

## Purpose

Assess impact and risk when changing existing systems.

## When to Use

- Entry Lane: Product or Engineering
- Risk Modes: HYBRID/SAFE
- Typical triggers: existing production capability, legacy integration, contract change

## Steps

1. Identify existing behavior.
2. Identify impacted components.
3. Identify consumers.
4. Check contracts/schemas/APIs/events.
5. Identify rollout strategy.
6. Identify rollback strategy.
7. Recommend risk mode and gates.

## Required Outputs

- impact-analysis.md
- lean-sdd.md, if needed
- rollout-plan.md
- rollback-plan.md
